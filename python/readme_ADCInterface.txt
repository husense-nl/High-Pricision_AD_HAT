IR108 Connections:
Purple, Clear: Ground  (AVSS on ADS1263)
Black: Excitation, connected to 5V of ADS1263 board
Red: Input, connected to IN0 of ADS1263 board

IR120 Connections:
Black, Clear: Ground  (AVSS on ADS1263)
Brown: Thermistor connected to IN2 of ADS1263 board
Green: Thermopile +ve connected to IN4 of ADS1263 board
White: Thermopile -ve connected to IN5 of ADS1263 board
Red: Excitation connected to 3V3 of ADS1263 board

Additional connections of ADS1263 board
ADC is configured in differential mode. So IN1, IN3 connected to ground (AVSS)
